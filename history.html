LIFE HISTORY

Nina is born!

February 27, 2005

Nina was born on February 27, 2005 in Seattle, Washington. Her parents decided to name her Nina because '27' in Japanese sounds similar to Nina. (Hence we celebrate the 27th of each month as Nina day!)

Nizi Project

2019 - 2020

Her journey began by flying off to Sendai for her regional auditions where she was told by J.Y. Park, "You're totally a star." With her stellar vocals, star quality, and certainly a lot of hard effort, she placed 9th to land a spot as the main vocalist of NiziU.

NiziU Debut

December 2, 2020

Nina debuts as a 9 member group NiziU, with their debut song Step and a Step having over 100 million views on its Music Video.

71st NHK Kohaku Singing Festival

December 31, 2020

NiziU makes their appearance on the NHK show as the fastest group since debuting, at only 29 days. They performed Make you happy.

NiziU Live With U 2022 Light it Up

Summer 2022

NiziU's first ever independent live concerts. Traveled around 7 cities and did 16 performances, in order of Fukuoka, Hokkaido, Miyagi, Tokyo, Osaka, Aichi, and concluding it with Hyogo. Nina performed a vocal performance with Riku and Miihi, singing Never Enough from The Greatest Showman. It was the best performance EVER period. Nina stated that one of her favorite moments are when she is performing in front of WithU in live concerts.

NiziU Live With U 2022 Burn it Up

Tokyo: 11/12~13 Osaka: 12/17~18 2022

After completing 16 arena performances for Light it Up, NiziU was the fastest girls group to perform on the Tokyo Dome since they have debuted. They performed their new songs, Blue Moon and Already Special for the first time in Tokyo Dome, showing off Nina's stellar vocals through these ballad songs. Nina performed the song Nobody alongside Rima and Miihi, for their special Nizi Project performance.
